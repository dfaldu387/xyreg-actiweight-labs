# Budget Dashboard Explanation

## Overview
The **Budget Dashboard** provides a company-wide financial overview that aggregates budget and actual costs across ALL products in your company portfolio - whether they are launched, in development, or in any other stage.

## What It Shows

### 1. Summary Metrics
- **Total Budget**: The sum of all planned/budgeted costs across all products and their development phases
- **Actual Spent**: The sum of all real expenditures that have been recorded
- **Variance**: The difference between actual and budget (positive = over budget, negative = under budget)
- **Products**: Count of all products that have budget data
- **Phases**: Total number of development phases across all products

### 2. Budget by Product Table
This table breaks down the budget for each individual product:
- Shows each product's total budget, actual costs, and variance
- Includes the number of phases per product
- Status indicator showing if the product is over or under budget

### 3. Budget by Phase Type (Chart)
Shows how costs are distributed across different development phases:
- Concept & Feasibility
- Design & Development  
- Verification & Validation
- Clinical Validation
- Regulatory Submission
- Design Transfer & Launch
- Post-Market Surveillance
- And other custom phases

### 4. Budget Distribution (Pie Chart)
Visual representation of the percentage breakdown by phase type.

## Important Notes

### This Dashboard Includes:
✅ **All products** (launched, in development, planned, etc.)
✅ **All development phases** (from concept to post-market surveillance)
✅ **Both budget and actual costs**
✅ **Products across all markets**

### This Dashboard Does NOT:
❌ Filter by launch status (it's comprehensive, not just launched products)
❌ Include revenue data (that's in the Commercial section)
❌ Show profit/loss (budget is about costs only)
❌ Filter by specific markets

## Common Questions

**Q: Does this only show launched products?**
A: No, it shows ALL products in your company portfolio regardless of their launch status.

**Q: What's the purpose of tracking all products together?**
A: It gives you a complete financial picture of your R&D and product development investments across your entire portfolio. This helps with:
- Overall company budget planning
- Resource allocation decisions
- Identifying which products or phases are consuming the most resources
- Tracking cost overruns at a company level

**Q: Where can I see revenue data?**
A: Revenue and sales data are in the **Commercial** section, not the Budget Dashboard.

**Q: Can I filter to see only certain products?**
A: Currently no, but you can click "View" on any product row to see detailed budget information for that specific product in its Milestones page.
