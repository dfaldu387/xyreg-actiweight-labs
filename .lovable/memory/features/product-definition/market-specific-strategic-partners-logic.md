# Memory: features/product-definition/market-specific-strategic-partners-logic
Updated: now

The 'Strategic Partners' section is a primary collapsible accordion in the 'Markets & Regulatory' tab (using the Building2 icon). It manages market-specific structured partner data (Distribution, Clinical, and Regulatory). For EU markets, it also contains the Notified Body configuration. To maintain vertical space, partners are displayed in a compact single-line format with a 'three-dot' (MoreHorizontal) menu for Edit and Delete actions. Editing is performed via a category-specific modal dialog (e.g., 'Clinical Partner'). Genesis Step 13 (Strategic Partners) requires at least 2 partners total across all selected markets and categories to be marked complete.
